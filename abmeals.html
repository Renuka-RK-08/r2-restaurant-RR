Jackfruit Biryani

Raw Jackfruit is great for diabetes. It is found that raw jackfruit has a much lower glycemic load than rice and wheat. That means, when you replace a cup of rice or two rotis with a cup of raw jackfruit, your blood glucose will not increase as much. Raw Jackfruit being a fibrous fruit leaves it's own amazing juicy flavors to the rice that gives an awesome aroma and flavour to this super filling one pot meal.
